Our Services

Dignified and Professional Repatriation Services Care

At AmaTshawe Funeral Directors, we provide compassionate and comprehensive repatriation services, ensuring your loved one is returned home with the utmost care, respect, and professionalism.

Domestic Repatriation

We cover any distance or remote area within South African borders. Our fleet is temperature and satellite controlled, with options for family co-travel.

Cross-Border Transport

We provide road transportation to neighboring countries (Namibia, Botswana, Zimbabwe, Lesotho, Eswatini, Mozambique), with drivers experienced in border protocols.

International Repatriation

Specialists in importing/exporting mortal remains globally. We navigate complex logistics to bring your loved one home with dignity.

End-to-End Support

From mortuary removals (homes, hospitals, government) to funeral coordination, coffins, tombstones, rural burials, and cremations—we guide you at every step.

Benefits of Our Service:

  • Our vehicles have temperature monitors to ensure refrigeration within the legislation.
  • We offer co-travelling for family members.
  • All vehicles are fitted with security tracking for customer safety and current updates on the progress.
  • We assist with removals from homes, private and government hospitals and mortuaries.
  • We support families with the registration of deaths and issuing of death certificates.
  • We facilitate long-distance funerals.
  • We offer embalming services according to export regulations.
  • We allow for a short prayer within a radius of 50 km.
  • Our fleet and cargo have third party insurance.
